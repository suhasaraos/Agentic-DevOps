Agent Use Cases Across the SDLC


Specify

  • Generate draft BRDs/FRDs from transcripts
  • Auto-extract epics, stories & criteria
  • Stakeholder requirements with traceability

Plan

  • AI proposes backlog structure & priorities
  • Scenario-based forecasting (best/likely/worst)
  • Instant impact analysis on scope changes

Build

  • Agents assist with design clarifications
  • Auto-link requirements to work items
  • See "feature completion likelihood" vs % complete

Test / Verify

  • Suggest test cases from requirements & risks
  • Auto-checks surface defects early
  • Real-time quality risk on the dashboards

Deploy

  • AI recommends rollout strategy (canary/blue-green/flags)
  • Predictive deployment risk signals
  • Live go/no-go indicators during release

Operate

  • SRE agents watch telemetry, auto-open tickets
  • Incidents summarized with root cause & impact
  • Business-impact views, not just CPU graphs
